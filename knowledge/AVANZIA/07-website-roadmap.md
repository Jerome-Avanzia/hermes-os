# AVANZIA Website Roadmap

## Goal

Build AVANZIA.tech as the central digital front door for the company.

## Pages

1. Home
2. Services
3. Hermes OS
4. Portfolio
5. About
6. Resources
7. Contact

## Homepage Goals

- Explain what AVANZIA is.
- Clearly differentiate AVANZIA from traditional agencies.
- Introduce Hermes as the operating system behind the company.
- Present services with clear business outcomes.
- Drive visitors to book a discovery call or start a project.

## Success Criteria

- Visitors understand AVANZIA in under 30 seconds.
- Clear call-to-action on every page.
- Consistent messaging across the site.
- Foundation ready for SEO expansion.
