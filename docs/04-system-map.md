## Domains

- **Identity** — people, AI agents, roles and access.
- **Memory** — durable context, history and retained learning.
- **Knowledge** — structured documentation, reference material and decisions.
- **Agents** — AI agents that perform defined responsibilities.
- **Skills** — reusable capabilities available to AI agents.
- **SOPs** — approved, repeatable operating procedures.
- **Businesses** — business entities, objectives and operating context.
- **Projects** — bounded initiatives, work plans and deliverables.
- **Integrations** — governed connections to external systems.
- **Automations** — repeatable workflows triggered by events or schedules.

## Domain Relationships

Identity governs all domains. Knowledge and memory inform AI agents. Skills and SOPs guide their work. Businesses and projects establish priorities, while integrations and automations connect execution to external systems.
