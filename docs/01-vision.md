## Long-Term Vision

Hermes OS becomes a coherent operating foundation through which people and AI agents can coordinate knowledge, work and business systems with increasing autonomy.

## Mission

Design a practical, secure and composable operating system for building and operating AI-enabled businesses.

## Goals

- Connect information, processes and tools through shared systems.
- Give AI agents clear context, governed capabilities and accountable workflows.
- Reduce operational friction while preserving human judgment.
- Create reusable foundations that scale across AVANZIA businesses.
