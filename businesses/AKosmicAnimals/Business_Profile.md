# Business Profile --- AKosmicAnimals

**Status:** Draft v1.0

## Purpose

AKosmicAnimals is the first production business managed by Hermes OS.
This document is the canonical business profile used by the Decision
Engine and Executive Brief.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Mission

Help cat lovers express the unique emotional connection they have with
their cats through minimalist, relatable apparel.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## North Star Goal

Reach **USD 1,000/month** in Etsy revenue, then scale through repeatable
systems and additional product lines.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Business Model

-   Platform: Etsy
-   Fulfilment: Printify (POD)
-   Primary Market: United States
-   Secondary Markets: UK, Canada, Australia

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Target Audience

-   Cat parents
-   Introverts
-   Remote workers
-   Book lovers
-   People who enjoy subtle, sarcastic cat humour

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Brand Positioning

Minimalist. Emotional. Not cartoonish. Premium but approachable.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Current Priorities

1.  Increase qualified traffic
2.  Improve conversion rate
3.  Grow Pinterest traffic
4.  Expand personalised listings
5.  Automate repetitive work

------------------------------------------------------------------------
