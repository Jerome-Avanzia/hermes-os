# AKosmicAnimals -- Business Discovery Profile

**Source:** Business discovery interview with the founder.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Mission

Help cat lovers express and celebrate the exceptional emotional
connection they have with their cats through minimalist apparel and
meaningful phrases.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# North Star Goal

Reach **USD 1,000/month** in Etsy revenue as the first major milestone.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Ideal Customer

Primary audience:

-   Cat parents
-   Introverts
-   Remote workers (WFH)
-   Book lovers
-   People who identify with quiet, sarcastic or emotionally relatable
    cat humor

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Competitive Advantage

-   Minimalist design style
-   Text-first concepts
-   Emotional connection instead of "cute" graphics
-   Premium understated aesthetic

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Current Bottlenecks

## Biggest Problem

-   Traffic
-   Conversion

The founder suspects Etsy visibility is limiting growth, but this
remains a hypothesis to validate with data.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Current Opportunities

-   Increase qualified traffic
-   Grow Pinterest
-   Expand personalized listings
-   Improve conversion rate

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Biggest Time Wasters

-   Etsy SEO work
-   Repetitive listing creation
-   Copy/paste between systems

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Repetitive Tasks Suitable for Automation

-   Listing preparation
-   Mockup management
-   Product synchronization
-   Copy generation
-   Metadata management

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Metrics Checked Daily

-   Views
-   Clicks
-   Favorites
-   Sales
-   Orders
-   Messages
-   Reviews

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Executive Brief Requirements

Each morning Hermes should answer:

1.  Is AKosmicAnimals progressing?
2.  Sales since yesterday?
3.  New orders?
4.  Messages requiring action?
5.  New reviews?
6.  KPI changes?
7.  Biggest opportunity today?
8.  Biggest risk?
9.  Top three recommended actions.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Success Criteria

Hermes helps the business owner spend less time managing the shop and
more time executing the highest-value work.
