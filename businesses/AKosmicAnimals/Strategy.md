# Strategy --- AKosmicAnimals

**Status:** Draft v1.0

## Strategic Objective

Build AKosmicAnimals into a repeatable, profitable cat-themed apparel
brand that serves as the reference business for Hermes OS.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 12-Month Goals

-   Reach USD 1,000/month Etsy revenue
-   Publish 100 high-quality listings
-   Establish Pinterest as a consistent traffic source
-   Launch personalized products
-   Build reusable operating procedures

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Strategic Pillars

### 1. Product Excellence

-   Minimalist, premium designs
-   Strong mockups
-   Consistent branding

### 2. Traffic Growth

-   Pinterest
-   Etsy SEO
-   Seasonal launches

### 3. Conversion Optimization

-   Better thumbnails
-   Better descriptions
-   Continuous A/B testing

### 4. Operational Efficiency

-   Hermes OS documentation
-   AI-assisted workflows
-   Standard operating procedures

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Current Priorities

1.  Finish Hermes documentation
2.  Expand product catalog
3.  Improve listing quality
4.  Measure KPIs weekly

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Risks

-   Spending too much time on systems instead of publishing products.
-   Inconsistent publishing cadence.
-   Depending on a single traffic source.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Review Cadence

-   Daily: Executive Brief
-   Weekly: KPI review
-   Monthly: Strategy review
